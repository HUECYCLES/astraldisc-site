General Ombudsman Office

The Hometown General Ombudsman Office aims to investigate complaints and concerns from Hometown residents as well as direct them to the proper resources for resolution.

Only send a message to the Complaint Response Team if:

  1. You believe you've experienced any kind of unfair treatment and/or poor service by Town Hall bodies;
  2. There is a serious transgression that you believe has been impacting the orderly town environment;
  3. You have contacted the proper authorities (see: Hometown Police Force) beforehand and you haven't been able to come closer to a resolution.

Do not send a message to the Complaint Response Team if:

  1. Your problem could easily be solved by contacting the Hometown Police Force;
  2. Your intent is to bother/harass the Mayor or her staff members;
  3. You are afflicted by personal issues that only impact YOU.

If your complaint meets these requirements, you can proceed with the form through the link below.
