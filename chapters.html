About the Project

ASTRAL DISC is a project made by a team of artists and developers working a set of fanmade DELTARUNE Chapters 3-8.

We are not affiliated with Toby Fox and his team in any way, and all the work seen here is purely made for fun, created with passion and respect for Toby's original DELTARUNE franchise.

We hope all of this will interest you in some way!
